About the App

Welcome the Kauai Water Data app. This app is a place to store water data for the island of Kauai focusing around Waimea Canyon. For my Thesis I was answering the question why Waimea Canyon has such a distinct shape from the other canyons on the islands.

Storing Data

There are three pages related to inputting and storing data; Table of Samples, Input New Samples, and Map of Water Data locations. The Input New Samples page is a form where you can submit information about your water samples. The Table of Samples is table of all the data that has been inputted. Finally, the Map of Water data location show the geographic spread of samples.

Other Data

There are two other maps on the app. The is a geologic map of the island and a map showing the Waimea Watershed and the DEM of the island.
