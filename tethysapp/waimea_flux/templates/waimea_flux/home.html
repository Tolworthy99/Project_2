Home

Welcome the Waimea Data app. This app is a place to store water and soil data for the island of Kauai focusing around Waimea Canyon. For my Thesis I was answering the question why Waimea Canyon is so distinct from the other canyons on the islands.

The Data

There is a map of the surface geology on the island. There is a map that holds a dem of the elevation on the island, the average annual rainfall and AET, as well as the polygon show Waimea canyon. There is page with the table of data and then finally a page for form submission to add more data.
